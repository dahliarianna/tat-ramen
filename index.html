TAT

ramen bar

Located in the vibrant city of Austin, Tat Ramen Bar emerges as a culinary haven born from a shared passion for traditional Japanese flavors and local Texan warmth. Founded by seasoned ramen enthusiasts with a deep-rooted respect for the craft, our journey began with a mission to bring genuine ramen experiences to the vibrant streets of Austin.

Rated best Japanese restaurant in Austin

crafted with locally sourced ingredients and fresh, farm-to-table produce

Drawing inspiration from the ramen stalls of Tokyo and Osaka, each recipe at Tat is a nod to authenticity, using locally sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques.
